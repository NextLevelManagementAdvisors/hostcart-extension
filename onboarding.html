Welcome to Hostcart Connector

Hostcart automates restocking for your short-term rental properties. This extension keeps your Instacart and Turno connections alive so you don't have to log back in every week.

  1. Open hostcart.nlma.io/settings/extension in a new tab and generate a 6-digit pairing code.
  2. Paste the code below and click Connect.
  3. Log into Instacart normally in any tab — the extension captures your session automatically.
  4. Log into Turno normally in any tab — same auto-capture.
  5. Close this tab. You're done.
